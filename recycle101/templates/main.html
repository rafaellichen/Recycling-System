How recycling in New York works?

Recycling in New York depends on two aspects

Item type

  • Recyable items such as paper, plastic, glass that is usually handled by the Department of Sanitation

  • Difficult to recycle items which is handled by the Department of Sanitation, however is a hassle for them. Items like electronics, home appliances, automotive, furniture etc.

  • Strict rules and regulations in-place by the DSNY for difficult to recycle items

  • Several programs are offered by DSNY such as legally mandated take-back programs to help NYC residents to despose of these items properly

  • Several donation centers available that accept these items

Item condition

  • Items in working condition will be accepted by Donation centers

  • Items that are not in working condition should be recycled through DSNY programs

  • Programs such as DSNY Safe Disposal Events accepts these difficult to recycle items

  • DSNY's Special Waste Drop Off Sites accepts these items
